Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Dad vs Twins AI Uploader ("we", "our", or "the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

When you connect your TikTok account using TikTok Login, we may receive basic account information such as your TikTok user ID, username, and access tokens required to publish content on your behalf.

We do not collect passwords. All authentication is handled securely by TikTok.

How We Use Information

  • To authenticate users via TikTok Login
  • To allow users to publish AI-generated videos after explicit approval
  • To maintain application functionality and security

Content Posting

No content is posted to TikTok without the user's explicit action and approval. The App does not post automatically or without user consent.

Data Storage

We do not sell or share user data with third parties. Any access tokens are stored securely and only for as long as necessary to provide the service.

Data Deletion

Users may revoke access at any time through their TikTok account settings. Upon revocation, all associated access data is deleted.

Children's Privacy

This application is intended for content creators and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.
